Stakeholders



The project brings together stakeholders from across the maritime industry to develop a comprehensive ranking system for container ports. The stakeholders include:

  • Port Operators
  • Ship Liners or Owners
  • Inland Logistics Providers
  • Government Bodies and Regulators
  • Maritime Port Authorities
  • Cargo Owners and Freight Forwarders

The stakeholders play a very important role in shaping our ranking criteria, contributing expert insights, and ensuring that the benchmarking reflect the latest in maritime excellence. Join us in driving the future of port operations:

  • Enhance Global Trade Efficiency
  • Promote Sustainable Maritime Practices
  • Foster Innovation and Resilience in Port Operations

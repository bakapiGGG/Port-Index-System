FAQ



Q: What is this ranking system?

A: A comprehensive ranking system evaluating global container ports based on efficiency, smartness, greenness, and resilience.

Q: How are the rankings determined?

A: Rankings are based on a mix of quantitative data (AIS, business statistics) and qualitative expert ratings on key performance indicators.

Q: Who contributes to the rankings?

A: Experts from various maritime sectors, including port operations, logistics, government, and environmental agencies.

Q: Can I access the rankings for free?

A: Yes, the rankings are publicly available on our website for industry stakeholders and the public.

Q: How often are the rankings updated?

A: The rankings are revised and updated annually to reflect the latest developments and performances.

Q: How can I join the expert pool?

A: Interested individuals can register on our website to be considered for the expert pool, subject to approval.

Q: What is the significance of the rankings?

A: The rankings aim to foster improvement in port operations globally, encouraging ports to adopt best practices and innovations.

Research



This research project initially in the testing mode intends to stand at the forefront of maritime innovation, developing the first comprehensive port index system that ranks container ports globally on efficiency, smartness, greenness, and resilience. This work not only sets new standards in port operations but also encourages ports to adopt best practices and technologies. The impact of our work promises to be far-reaching through:



  • Driving Operational Excellence: Encouraging ports to improve efficiency and embrace automation.

  • Promoting Sustainability: Highlighting the importance of green practices in reducing maritime environmental footprints.

  • Enhancing Resilience: Preparing ports to better manage risks and recover from disruptions.
